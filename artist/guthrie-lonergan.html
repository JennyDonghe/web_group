Guthrie Lonergan

Artist | Database aesthetics, lists, “found web” structure

Overview

Guthrie Lonergan works with the vernacular of the web: directories, index pages, link lists, and default UI. His projects often treat browsing, cataloging, and re-contextualizing as an artistic medium, highlighting how structure and navigation shape meaning online.
